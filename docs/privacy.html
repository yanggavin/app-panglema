FatYet Privacy Journey

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

At FatYet (胖了吗 - "Are You Fat Yet?"), we believe your body journey is yours and yours alone. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled—or rather, how we prioritize not collecting it.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

FatYet is designed as a privacy-first application. We operate on a "Local-First" principle, meaning your data stays where it belongs: on your device.

  • ✅ No user accounts required
  • ✅ No email collection
  • ✅ No server-side data storage
  • ✅ No third-party tracking
  • ✅ No advertising networks

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • Personal Identifiers: No name, email, phone number, or address
  • Location Data: No GPS tracking or location services
  • Device Information: No device IDs or advertising identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: No analytics tools or user behavior tracking
  • Contact Information: No access to your contacts

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

3.1 Body Check-In Data

  • Weight measurements (if you choose to track weight)
  • Body sensation ratings (using our playful slider)
  • Mood tags (Fluidity, Stillness, Deep Rest, etc.)
  • Personal notes and reflections
  • Date and time of each check-in

3.2 Photos

  • Photos you choose to attach to check-ins are stored in the app's private container
  • Photos are encrypted and not accessible to other apps
  • We never transmit or back up photos to any external server

3.3 User Preferences

  • Nickname (optional, stored locally)
  • Weight unit preference (kg/lbs)
  • Daily reminder settings
  • Privacy settings (hide weight feature)

4. Apple HealthKit Integration

If you choose to enable Apple Health integration:

  • Read Access: FatYet may read your weight and step count data from Apple Health (only if you grant permission)
  • Write Access: FatYet may write weight data to Apple Health (only if you grant permission)
  • Privacy Guarantee: All HealthKit data remains on your device and is governed by Apple's HealthKit privacy policies
  • No Transmission: We never transmit HealthKit data to our servers or third parties

Important: You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > FatYet.

5. Data Storage

FatYet is designed as a local-only application. All your data stays on your device:

  • ✅ Data stored using SwiftData (Apple's local database)
  • ✅ Encrypted by iOS's Data Protection
  • ✅ Protected by your device passcode
  • ✅ Included in your device backups (iTunes/Finder/iCloud device backup)
  • ❌ NO cloud sync to any server

Note: We believe in keeping your personal health data private and local. Cloud features are not planned.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that collect, track, or process your data. This includes:

  • ❌ No analytics (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • ❌ No crash reporting (e.g., Crashlytics, Sentry)
  • ❌ No advertising networks
  • ❌ No social media SDKs
  • ❌ No cloud services (no iCloud in v1.0)
  • ❌ No location services

7. Data Retention and Deletion

7.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you control how long your data is retained.

7.2 Deleting Your Data

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Delete Individual Check-Ins: Swipe left on any check-in in Timeline view
  • Delete All Data: Go to Settings > Delete All Data (this action is irreversible)
  • Delete the App: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data

Note: Your device backups (iTunes/Finder/iCloud device backup) may contain app data. Delete device backups separately if needed.

8. Children's Privacy

FatYet is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the app, please contact us.

9. International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your data remains in your physical location.

10. Your Rights

Under various privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you have rights regarding your personal data. Since FatYet stores all data locally on your device:

  • Right to Access: You have full access to all your data in the app
  • Right to Deletion: You can delete your data at any time through Settings
  • Right to Portability: You can access all your data within the app
  • Right to Rectification: You can edit any check-in data within the app

11. Security

We take security seriously:

  • Local Storage: Data is stored in iOS's secure app sandbox
  • Photo Encryption: Photos are encrypted using iOS's Data Protection
  • Device Security: Your device passcode protects all app data
  • No Network Transmission: Since we don't send data anywhere, there's no risk of interception
  • Apple's Security: Leverages iOS's built-in encryption and security features

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Displaying an in-app notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of FatYet after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Medical Disclaimer

Important: FatYet is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. The app is designed for mindful self-tracking and awareness purposes only. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional before making any health-related decisions.

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
