üè• Apple Health not syncing?

Make sure you've granted Panglema permission in the iOS Health app:

  1. Open the Health app
  2. Tap your profile icon (top right)
  3. Go to Apps > Panglema
  4. Enable permissions for Weight and Steps

You can also request authorization again through Panglema's Settings screen.
