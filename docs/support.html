Make sure you've granted FatYet permission in the iOS Health app:

ğŸ” How is my data protected?

Your data is protected through multiple layers of security:

  • Device passcode: iOS secures all app data with your device passcode
  • App sandbox: iOS isolates app data from other apps
  • Encryption: Photos and data are encrypted using iOS Data Protection
  • No cloud sync: Data never leaves your device, eliminating cloud security risks
